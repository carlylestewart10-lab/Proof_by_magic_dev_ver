About Proof by Magic

Proof by Magic is a game made for everyone. The gameplay is inspired by the process of writing proofs in mathematics. On an intuitive level, a proof is simply a very convincing logical argument for why a certain statement is true. However, over the centuries, mathematicians have been able to describe in greater and greater detail what it means to prove.

For many people, when they are exposed to mathematics, the process of coming up with a proof seems impossible, and is often quite stressful. The purpose of this game is to teach this skill, in a fun and engaging environment.
