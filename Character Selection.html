Görit
Görit is an earthen sorcerer. He lives by himself in a hut in the forest, and is an expert on tree-like structures
Adarassa
Adarassa is a water nymph. She has the ability to control the movements of water, and has an incredible intuitive understanding of shapes and spaces
Nyaya
